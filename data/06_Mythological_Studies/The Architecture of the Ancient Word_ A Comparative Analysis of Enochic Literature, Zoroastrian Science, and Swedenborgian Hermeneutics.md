## **Executive Summary**

The recovery of the Ethiopic Book of Enoch (*1 Enoch*) in 1773 and the subsequent discovery of Aramaic Enochic fragments at Qumran in 1947 precipitated a theological and historiographical crisis. These texts, clearly influential on the New Testament writers, present a cosmology that is neither strictly Mosaic nor Hellenistic, but deeply rooted in the scientific and mythological soil of the Ancient Near East. This report investigates the hypothesis that *1 Enoch* represents a spiritually guided restoration of a primordial "Ancient Word"‚Äîa corpus of wisdom preceding the Hebraic canon‚Äîrather than a mere literal rediscovery or syncretic invention.

Utilizing the theological framework of Emanuel Swedenborg (1688‚Äì1772), we validate the proposition that "Enoch" signifies a collective scribal community tasked with collecting the perceptive wisdom of the "Most Ancient Church," while "Noah" represents the doctrinal vessel constructed to preserve this wisdom through a catastrophic spiritual inundation. We examine the historical transmission of this wisdom through the Zoroastrian Magi and the Aramaic-speaking scribes of the Achaemenid Empire, arguing that the so-called "scientific" texts of the East (such as the *DƒÅmdƒÅd Nask*) constitute the perverted remnants of this Ancient Word.

Furthermore, we evaluate the claim that the scribes of *1 Enoch* engaged in a process of "mythic bricolage" guided by spiritual influx. By cross-referencing modern Near-Death Experience (NDE) research‚Äîwhich authenticates the "Divine Human" archetype‚Äîwith the scribal "correction" of Mesopotamian astronomy, we demonstrate that the Enochic literature functions as a "re-consecrated" vessel. It strips ancient science of its polytheistic determinism to reveal the "Science of Correspondences," the immutable law binding natural effects to spiritual causes.

## **I. The Epistemological Fracture: Defining the Ancient Word**

To understand the function of *1 Enoch* as a vehicle of restoration, one must first define the substrate it intends to restore. The modern historiographical consensus views the progression of religion as linear: from animism to polytheism to monotheism. However, the Swedenborgian hermeneutic, supported by the internal testimony of Ancient Near Eastern (ANE) texts, posits a devolutionary model. It argues that a primordial, integrated worldview‚Äîwhere the natural world was legible as a spiritual text‚Äîwas fractured, leading to the separation of "science" from "theology." This chapter establishes the definitions of the "Enoch" and "Noah" entities, not as biological ancestors, but as typological eras of human consciousness.

### **1.1 The Most Ancient Church and the Age of Perception**

Swedenborg‚Äôs exegesis in *Arcana Coelestia* posits that the earliest phase of human spirituality, termed the "Most Ancient Church" (symbolized by Adam), was characterized by a cognitive state of "Perception" (*perceptio*). This state was defined by the seamless unity of the Will and the Understanding.1

#### **1.1.1 The Mechanism of Internal Respiration**

The cognitive architecture of this "Golden Age" humanity was fundamentally distinct from modern consciousness. They possessed "internal respiration," a state where their breathing was inextricably linked to the rhythm of their thought and the influx of the Spirit.2 This allowed for a direct, unmediated communication with the spiritual world. They did not require an external, written law because the laws of the universe were inscribed upon their "internal man."

When a member of this community observed a natural object‚Äîa mountain, a lamb, or the sun‚Äîthey did not merely categorize it empirically. They instantly perceived its spiritual correspondence. A mountain was not just geology; it was the "elevation of the heart to the Lord" (Celestial Love). The sun was not just a ball of plasma; it was the Divine Love itself in an external form.3 This "representative language" meant that the entire cosmos served as a living Bible. The epistemological structure was "top-down": the spiritual cause was perceived first, and the natural object was understood merely as its effect.

#### **1.1.2 The Fall and the Necessity of the "Enoch" Function**

The "Fall" is interpreted not as a single moral transgression but as a catastrophic cognitive shift. As the "Most Ancient" people began to appropriate their perception as their own power (the rise of the *proprium* or selfhood), the unity of Will and Understanding was severed. The internal way was closed to prevent the profanation of truth. This crisis necessitated a new medium of revelation. If humanity could no longer *perceive* truth via the Will, they had to *learn* truth via the Understanding.4

This necessity birthed the "Enoch" function.

### **1.2 Enoch as Community: The First Librarians of the Spirit**

Swedenborg explicitly rejects the literal identification of Enoch as a single historical individual in the early chapters of Genesis. Instead, "Enoch" signifies a "community" or a specific "branch of the church" tasked with a monumental preservation effort.4 This redefinition validates the hypothesis that the *1 Enoch* texts are the product of a "scribal school" rather than a lone prophet.

* **The Mission:** The Enoch community recognized that the perceptive faculty was dying out. To prevent the wisdom of the Golden Age from vanishing entirely, they undertook the task of collecting the correspondences that the Most Ancient people perceived and reducing them into a doctrinal system.4  
* **The Transformation:** "Enoch walked with God; and he was not, for God took him" (Gen 5:24). Spiritually, this signifies that this specific mode of perception (walking with God) ceased to exist as a living experience ("he was not") but was preserved for future use ("God took him").4  
* **The Output:** The result of the Enoch community‚Äôs labor was the "Ancient Word" (*Verbum Vetustum*). This was a written corpus, composed entirely in the language of correspondences, intended to serve the coming "Ancient Church" (Noah).7

This definition validates the "Enochic" literary tradition's obsession with lists, taxonomies, and astronomical data. *1 Enoch* is not a narrative of a life; it is a *catalog* of the cosmos. This fits perfectly with Swedenborg's description of the Enoch community as "collectors" who reduced perception into doctrine.

### **1.3 Noah as Doctrine: The Ark of Conscience**

If Enoch is the *collector*, Noah is the *container*. The "Flood" represents the inundation of the human mind by falsities and lusts arising from the corrupted Will.5 The "Ark" represents the new cognitive structure‚Äîthe separation of the Will and the Understanding‚Äîconstructed to navigate this inundation.

* **Conscience:** The "Noachic" dispensation was established for a new type of human who required "conscience" formed by "external revelation".9 Because the Will was corrupted, the Lord separated the Understanding from it. This allowed humanity to intellectually learn truths (via the Ancient Word preserved by Enoch) and force their Will to obey them.  
* **The Ark‚Äôs Dimensions:** The intricate details of the Ark‚Äîits three stories, its window, its pitch‚Äîare not nautical specifications but correspondential descriptions of this new human mind (Natural, Rational, Spiritual degrees) protected from the flood of the *proprium*.6

## **II. The Zoroastrian Lineage: The Perversion of the Scientific Nasks**

The "Ancient Word" did not vanish without a trace. Swedenborg locates its remnants in "Great Tartary" (Central Asia/Iran).8 This geographical marker points directly to the Indo-Iranian sphere and the rise of Zoroastrianism. We posit that Zoroastrianism represents the historical institutionalization of the Noachic/Ancient Church, and that its history reveals the "perversion" of the Enochic wisdom.

### **2.1 The Geography of Wisdom: Great Tartary and the Avesta**

Swedenborg‚Äôs assertion that the Ancient Word is preserved among the "Tartars" and "Mogols" 8 aligns with the provenance of the Avesta. The *Vendidad* locates the origin of the faith in *Airyana Vaejah*, a region identified with the steppes of Central Asia.11

The Zoroastrian canon, specifically the lost Sasanian Avesta, was divided into three categories: *Gathic* (liturgical), *Dat* (legal), and *Hadha-manthric* (scientific/scholarly).12 This third category, particularly the **DƒÅmdƒÅd Nask**, serves as the primary artifact of the "Enochic" science.

### **2.4 The Prophetic Restoration: Zarathushtra and Daniel**

The lineage was not wholly lost. The document 13 and 13 identify two key moments of "correction" within the Magian line itself:

1. **Zarathushtra (Zoroaster):** He reformed the "Magic" by restoring monotheism (*Ahura Mazda*) and clarifying the ethical dualism (*Asha* vs. *Druj*). He stripped the nature-gods (*Daevas*) of their divinity, reclassifying them as demons, thus restoring the distinction between the Holy and the Profane.13  
2. **Daniel:** As a Jewish exile, Daniel was appointed **Rab-signin** ("Chief of the Prefects," Dan. 2:48) and **Rab-chartumayya** ("Chief of the Magicians," Dan. 4:9). While often conflated with the specific title **Rab-mag** (held by Nergal-sharezer in Jer. 39:3), Daniel's functional authority over the Babylonian wise men was absolute. Crucially, this status was not granted based on technical ritual proficiency, but on his superior **pneumatic capacity** (*ruach yattira* or "excellent spirit") found within him (Dan. 5:12). ## **III. The Aramaic Nexus: The Mechanism of Synthesis**

The transmission of this "Ancient Word" (perverted by the Magi, restored by Daniel) to the scribes of *1 Enoch* was facilitated by a specific technological and linguistic infrastructure: the **Imperial Aramaic** administration of the Achaemenid Empire.

### **3.1 Imperial Aramaic as the "Imperial Lattice"**

Aramaic was the "nervous system" of the Persian Empire.12 It functioned as the interface between the Jewish scribal elites of Yehud and the scientific archives of the Magi.

* **The "Demilitarized Zone" of Language:** Because Aramaic was a secular administrative language (distinct from the liturgical Avestan), it acted as a "neutral container".12 Jewish scribes like Ezra could read Aramaic summaries of Zoroastrian science without engaging in the idolatrous liturgy of the *Yasna*.  
* **Heterograms (*Huzwarish*):** The writing system itself enforced a cognitive synthesis. Scribes wrote Aramaic words (e.g., *MLK'*) but read them aloud as Persian (*Shah*).12 This "heterographic" literacy meant that Jewish scribes were mentally translating Persian concepts into Semitic categories with every stroke of the pen.

### **3.2 The Smoking Gun: The Aramaic Enoch Fragments (4Q208‚Äì211)**

The hypothesis that *1 Enoch* draws from this Aramaic/Persian stream is empirically verified by the Dead Sea Scrolls. The Aramaic fragments of the *Astronomical Book* contain undeniable fingerprints of Mesopotamian science:

* **The Linear Zigzag Function:** The texts calculate the length of daylight using a specific ratio (2:1 longest to shortest day).3 This ratio is geographically accurate for **Babylon (32¬∞N)** but inaccurate for **Jerusalem (31¬∞N)**. The retention of this "Babylonian math" proves that the scribes were copying data directly from the Aramaic archives of the East.12  
* **The "Gates" Terminology:** The description of the sun rising through distinct "Gates" (*bƒÅb*) is a direct calque of the Akkadian terminology found in the *MUL.APIN* series.12

However, the Jewish scribes did not merely copy; they "corrected."

## **IV. 1 Enoch as the Restored Vessel: Mythic Bricolage and Correction**

The composition of *1 Enoch* represents a deliberate, spiritually guided program of **"Mythic Bricolage"**.12 The scribes dismantled the "perverted" science of the Magi and reconstructed it within a monotheistic framework, effectively restoring the "Ancient Word" to its original function.

### **4.1 The Astronomical Correction: From Fate to Service**

The *Astronomical Book* (1 En 72‚Äì82) is often dismissed as "pseudoscientific" because of its 364-day calendar. However, under Swedenborgian hermeneutics, this is a "Theology of State".3

* **Solar (364) vs. Lunar (354):** The text champions a solar calendar where the year is perfectly divisible by 7 (364 \= 52 x 7). In correspondences, the **Sun** signifies **Divine Love** (Celestial) and the **Number 7** signifies the **Holy State/Sabbath**.3  
* **The "Lagging" Moon:** The text critiques the lunar calendar for "falling behind" the sun by 10 days. Spiritually, the **Moon** corresponds to **Faith/Truth** (Spiritual). The polemic asserts that a church ruled only by Faith (Lunar/Jewish) "lags behind" the perfect order of a church ruled by Love (Solar/Enochian).3  
* **The "Correction" of Agency:** Unlike the *Enuma Anu Enlil*, where stars control fate, *1 Enoch* presents the stars as "obedient servants." They follow the "command of the Lord." The scribes stripped the astrological determinism (Magian perversion) and restored the "Science of Correspondences" (the order of nature reflects the Will of God).12

### **4.2 The "Son of Man" Controversy: Resolving the Identity**

A central theological tension in *1 Enoch* is the identification of Enoch himself as the "Son of Man" in *Parables of Enoch* (1 En 71:14). Traditional scholarship views this as a heretical apotheosis. However, the Swedenborgian "Representative" framework resolves this.3

* **Enoch as Doctrine:** As established, "Enoch" represents the "Collected Doctrine" of the Ancient Word.  
* **Son of Man as Divine Truth:** The title "Son of Man" corresponds to the "Divine Truth" or "The Word".3  
* **The Resolution:** When the angel declares to Enoch, "You are that Son of Man," it is not elevating a human to godhood. It is a theological tautology: **"This Doctrine (Enoch) is the Divine Truth (Son of Man)."** The text describes the *glorification* of the Ancient Word‚Äîthe realization that the doctrinal container (Enoch) is filled with the Divine Truth itself.3

### **4.3 The Animal Apocalypse: Preserving the Dispensational History**

The *Animal Apocalypse* (1 En 85‚Äì90) retells history using animal symbols (Bulls and Sheep). This is not arbitrary allegory but a rigorous application of correspondences.3

* **White Bull (Adam/Enoch):** Represents the **Celestial State** (Innocence/Will) of the Most Ancient Church.  
* **Sheep (Jacob/Israel):** Represents the **Spiritual State** (Charity/Faith) of the Ancient/Jewish Church.  
* **New White Bull (Messiah):** The eschaton brings a return to the "White Bull," signifying the restoration of the Celestial State (the New Jerusalem).

The scribes successfully preserved the internal history of the church‚Äîthe shift from Will (Bull) to Conscience (Sheep) and back to Will (Bull)‚Äîconfirming their access to the "Ancient Word" lineage.3
