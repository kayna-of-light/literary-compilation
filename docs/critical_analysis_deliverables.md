## Key Themes Across Documents

### Theme 1: Unfalsifiability Problem

**Pervasive Issue**: Core theological claims cannot be empirically tested
- Correspondences (any text can be read allegorically)
- Divine influx (indistinguishable from natural meaning-making)
- CDE (any outcome explainable post-hoc)
- Ancient Word (no text exists; unfalsifiable by definition)
- Volunteer Souls (no verification method)

**Solution**: Either specify what would falsify OR acknowledge as faith commitments

---

### Theme 2: Circular Reasoning

**Pattern**: Framework uses Swedenborgian premises to validate Swedenborgian conclusions

**Example Chain**:
1. Accept Swedenborg's correspondences as revelatory
2. Apply correspondences to analyze Exodus
3. Find Exodus fits regeneration pattern
4. Conclude: Swedenborg was right!

**Problem**: Presupposes what it sets out to prove

**Solution**: Provide independent validation mechanism for correspondence doctrine

---

### Theme 3: Epistemological Confusion

**Current Claim**: "Two-tiered epistemology integrating faith and reason"

**Reality**: Theological interpretation layered onto accepted scientific findings

**Confusion**: Conflating hermeneutic stance with knowledge generation method

**Solution**: Be explicit that this is **theology informing interpretation**, not **science validating theology**

---

### Theme 4: Methodological Asymmetry

**Observation**: Historical-critical method applied to Bible but not to Swedenborg

**Double Standard**:
- Genesis sources deconstructed (J, E, P, D)
- Swedenborg's *Arcana* accepted as revelatory

**Options**:
- **A**: Apply HCM to Swedenborg (analyze cultural influences, psychological factors)
- **B**: Acknowledge Swedenborg is faith commitment (honest about asymmetry)

**Recommendation**: Option B for intellectual honesty

---

### Theme 5: Evidence Overreach

**Pattern**: Claims exceed what data can support

**Examples**:
- "Volunteer souls" (unfalsifiable)
- "14,000-year songlines" (contested; overstated)
- "Paul = proprium" (theological judgment, not diagnosis)
- "Ancient Word fragments in Genesis" (speculative, not empirical)

**Solution**: Distinguish confidence levels throughout framework

---

### Theme 6: Alternative Theories Underdeveloped

**Pattern**: Competing explanations dismissed without adequate refutation

**Key Alternatives**:
- CDE ‚Üí Dual Inheritance Theory (Boyd & Richerson)
- Ancient Word ‚Üí Universal cognition + convergent evolution
