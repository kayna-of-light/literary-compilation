## Executive Summary

**OVERALL RATING: PARTIALLY RELIABLE**

The document is a well-structured synthesis of mainstream critical scholarship on the Book of Daniel. ## 6. FRAMEWORK IMPLICATIONS

### 6.1 Critical Conflict with Knowledge Graph Node SWED-043

**MAJOR ISSUE IDENTIFIED**

The current knowledge graph contains node `SWED-043` titled "Daniel as Rab-mag (Prophetic-Sapiential Synthesis)" with status "validated" and confidence "high." This node states:

> "The historical-theological significance of Daniel's appointment as RAB-MAG (Chief of the Magi/Wise Men) under Nebuchadnezzar (Dan 2:48, 5:11)."

**The document under review correctly demonstrates that:**
1. The term "Rab-mag" appears ONLY in Jeremiah 39:3, 39:13, NOT in Daniel
2. Daniel's actual title is "Rab-signīn" (Chief Prefect) and "Rab-hartummin" (Chief of Magicians)
3. These are lexically distinct terms
4. The historical Rab-mag was Nergal-sharezer, not Daniel

**RECOMMENDATION:** Node SWED-043 should be revised to:
- Change title to "Daniel as Rab-hartummin (Chief of Wise Men)"
- Lower confidence from "high" to "moderate" 
- Add caveat about compositional dating
- Correct biblical citations
