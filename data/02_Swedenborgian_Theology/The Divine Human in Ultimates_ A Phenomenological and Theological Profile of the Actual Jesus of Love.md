* **Khat H** was told emphatically by Jesus that she had to return because she was put here to "perform great works" and "no one else could do them".21  
* **Keith J** was commanded, "Now go back and save your people."

These accounts reveal that the "Jesus of Love" is a Commander. He deploys "Volunteer Souls" to inject high-coherence memes (Love, Unity, Forgiveness) into the collective consciousness to counteract the fragmentation of the *proprium*.

### **10.2 The Profile of the Volunteer**

These individuals often exhibit a specific psychological profile:

* **Pre-birth Memories:** Recalling the "Covenant" made with the Jesus of Love before incarnation (Anamnesis).  
* **Alienation:** A sense of not belonging to the "social fragmentation" of the world.  
* **Mission Drive:** A compulsion to serve, heal, or teach.

The "Actual Jesus" is the Commander of this "silent army" of lovers. He empowers them not with authority, but with the capacity to love in a loveless world.

He invites the scientist to see the "Divine Bricolage" in the strata of history, the historian to see the "Bedrock Facts" as spiritual footprints, and the phenomenologist to see the "Being of Light" in the data of consciousness.

## **12\. Conclusion: The Actual Jesus**

The profile that emerges from this exhaustive synthesis is coherent, compelling, and radically distinct from both the "Historical Jesus" of the critics and the "Gnostic Christ" of the esoterics.

The **Actual Jesus of Love** is:

1. **The Culmination of Influx:** The final, perfect vessel forged by the Divine Bricolage through the debris of human history.  
2. **The Victor:** The One who conquered the *proprium* (self-love) through the "bedrock facts" of His life and death (Glorification).  
3. **The Divine Natural:** The One who eats the "broiled fish" of natural truth and sanctifies it, proving that matter and spirit are united in Him.  
4. **The Gardener:** The intimate cultivator of the individual soul, who knows us by name.  
5. **The Being of Light:** The objective, hyper-real personality encountered in NDEs, characterized by absolute, non-judgmental love.  
6. **The Lord of Work:** The active director of human evolution who commissions Volunteer Souls to serve the whole.

He is the "Divine Inflow into humanity." He is the proof that the universe is not a cold, fragmented accident, but a purposeful project of Love, anchored in history, verified by experience, and accessible to every heart that lives a life of charity. He is the Actual Jesus.
